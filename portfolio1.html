About the artist

Creating through
observation & quiet

I work at the intersection of painting and photography, drawn to the ordinary moments that hold something luminous — a window at dusk, still water, the geometry of daily life.

Based in New York, I've shown work across the northeast and accept commissions for both painted and photographic pieces. Each work is made slowly, with attention to light.
