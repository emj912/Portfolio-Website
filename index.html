About the artist

Creating through
observation & quiet

I work at the intersection of painting and photography, drawn to the ordinary moments that hold something luminous — a window at dusk, still water, the geometry of daily life.

Based in New York. I am a current student at Barnard College of Columbia University, interested in studying mathematics.
